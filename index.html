Gail Camp

Champion Duck Caller

Who is Gail Camp?

  • Born in Memphis, Tn on July 30, 1925.
  • She began her journey as a duck hunter by her Husbands side.
  • Her Husband, Frank Lee Camp, Jr, was her High school sweetheart.

Her Journey Duck Hunting

  • She Started out duck calling Because there was no room in the hunting blind to shoot a gun in between the Men she was around.
  • She never missed out on time spent with her husband ever again.
  • Not long after, she became so good at calling that the men required her presences on every hunt they went on.
  • The couple joined a hunting and fishing club in OK in Wiener, Arkansas.
  • Together they built a cabin that is still enjoyed by their families today.
  • Frank suggested to Gail that she should enter a calling contest to which she got in contact with many different members of the hunting community.
  • 1959 and 1960 Gail then went on to win the Women’s state Calling Awards in Tennessee as well as the Gulf Coast Championships.
  • After that she eventually would win the Woman’s World Championships in Stuttgart both Years.
  • In 1961 Gail slowed down on her competing due to having her daughter yet still went on to win the Herb Parsons International Championship in 1962.
  • Gail has been Passionate about Hunting with Golden Retrievers since her and her husband became involved with hunting.
  • Gail is passionate about being in the outdoors and is surrounded by Duck hunting Decor in her home including her own ducks that she has stuffed herself from years ago
  • This Year she will be turning 100 years old and will carry on the title of being the world oldest duck caller.
“A duck call in the hands of the unskilled is one of conservation's greatest assets.” - Nash Buckingham, De Shootinest Gent'man, 1941
